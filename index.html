A Letter For My Bunny

My dearest Bunny,

Happy 21st Birthday, my love. Twenty-one years of you existing in this world. I know birthdays can feel like just another day, but I hope you feel the weight of this one, you've been here for 21 years and that is something to be proud of.

You don't know how much I am proud of you, hindi lang sa big wins or small wins, but for the quiet battles. For staying in this world for 21 years despite the challenges, the bad days and mga moments na feel mo hindi mo na kaya. Sobrang daming nangyaring bagay na nangyari pero at the end of the day, you're still standing strong. I love how you're still you.

There are so many versions of you that I love, the way you laugh lalo na pag magkasama tayo. The way you deeply care about things. The way you smile and the way you look at me. It feels comforting whenever I look at you and see my future with you. You feel like home and I don't ever want to live elsewhere.

Hindi natin alam ang mangyayari sa taon na to pero I want to be here for all of it, honey. Sa mga bad days, sa mga masasayang araw, sa mga celebrations at kahit sa mga slow at boring na mga araw. I'll promise na lagi ko ipaparamdam sayo na you are a treasure to me, you are already something if in case you forget. I promise to love you sa lahat ng parte ng buhay mo/natin.

I hope this year brings us good luck, good fortune and maging dream na ayaw mong magising. I pray that everything you hoped for, and everything you wish comes true. You are the love of my life, my favorite person and the center of my universe.

Happy Birthday, Bunny.

You are my bunny.

HAPPY BIRTHDAY, BUNNY.
MY SWEETHEART, MY BUNGEE GUM, MY HOME, MY EVERYTHING.


And will always be my bunny.

From: Bubba
